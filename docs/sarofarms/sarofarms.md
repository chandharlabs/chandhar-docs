## Saro Farms


Saro Farms is the Agricultural Research and Development unit of Chandhar Research Labs, located in Arupathy, Mayiladuthurai, Tamil Nadu, India.

Saro Farms focuses on applied agricultural research, agri-technology innovation, and field-level experimentation. Through Saro Farms, Chandhar Research Labs aims to bridge the gap between scientific research and practical agriculture by 
developing and validating solutions in crop management, soil health, agri-IoT systems, livestock monitoring, and environmentally responsible farming practices.
