# Workshops

Chandhar Research Labs Pvt Ltd conducts **hands‑on, research‑oriented workshops**
for students, faculty, researchers, and industry professionals in the
domains of **5G, AI‑driven wireless systems, and advanced communication technologies**.

---



- [**Deep Learning for Wireless**](dl-wireless.md):
An intensive hands‑on training programme focusing on applying
machine learning and deep learning techniques to wireless communication systems.  
- [**5G Hands‑On**](5g-hands-on.md):
A practical, end‑to‑end workshop on 5G networks covering
RAN, Core, network slicing, MEC, and real testbeds.
