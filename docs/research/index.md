# Research

Research at **Chandhar Research Labs Pvt Ltd** focuses on
next‑generation wireless communication systems, intelligent networks,
and secure, spectrum‑efficient technologies.

---

## Research Areas

- [**5G Use Cases**](areas/5g-use-cases.md):
Research on practical and emerging applications of 5G networks,
including industry, smart infrastructure, and mission‑critical systems.  
- [**5G Security**](areas/5g-security.md):
Security, privacy, and resilience analysis of 5G RAN, Core,
and network slicing architectures.  
- [**5G Enabled Drones**](areas/5g-drones.md):
Research on UAV communication systems integrated with 5G and Beyond‑5G
networks for control, telemetry, and edge intelligence.  
- [**Deep Learning for Wireless**](areas/dl-wireless.md):
Application of machine learning and deep learning techniques to
wireless PHY, MAC, spectrum sensing, and network optimization.  
- [**Spectrum Management**](areas/spectrum-management.md):
Efficient and intelligent spectrum utilization using cognitive radio,
dynamic spectrum access, and policy‑driven allocation.  
- [**IoT Security**](areas/iot-security.md):
Security frameworks and intrusion detection mechanisms for large‑scale
IoT and machine‑type communication networks.
