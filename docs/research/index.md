# Research

Research at Chandhar Research Labs focuses on
next‑generation wireless communication systems, intelligent networks,
and secure, spectrum‑efficient technologies.

---

## Research Areas

- **5G Use Cases**:
Research on practical and emerging applications of 5G networks,
including industry, smart infrastructure, and mission‑critical systems.  
- 5G Security**:
Security, privacy, and resilience analysis of 5G RAN, Core,
and network slicing architectures.  
- IoT Security**:
Security frameworks and intrusion detection mechanisms for large‑scale
IoT and machine‑type communication networks.
